My Interest


Anna is interested in fine art, classic art, digital art, Graphic Design, and User Interface Design (she has a UI website dedicated to all of her UI design projects). She used to love drawing on paper, but ever since she started learning digital design, she spends most of her time drawing digitally. Her favorite program to draw and design is Adobe Illustrator.


In terms of music, Anna loves listening to a wide variety of music genres: Pop, Indie, Folk, Soft rock, Classic, Country, Film score, EDM, Jazz, Blue, and K-pop. She is a Swiftie and loves listening to Taylor Swift's music almost on a daily basis. Anna is a secret dancing-lover. When the right music is played, she will dance carelessly all night.


Besides listening and drawing, Anna also likes playing video games. Once she finds a really interesting video game, she will play it non-stop. Anna considers herself a casual gamer. She loves playing easy casual games that don't require strategy or technical thinking. Mobile game is her all-time favorite, notably Sky: Children of The Light and PlayTogether. However, she is interested in games with a controller as well, notably Baldur's Gate 3.
