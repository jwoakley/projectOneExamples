Mariam Diakite was born September 13th, 2002 Baton Rouge, LA (making her a Virgo sun). She has lived in various places, but currently attends the University of San Francisco. Mariam is a second year design major and film studies minor. Mariam loves to dance all forms, ballet, hip hop, contemporary… She loves movies, her favorites being La Haine, Grand Budapest Hotel, The Royal Tenenbaums, We Need to Talk About Kevin, Superbad, Moonlight, Do The Right Thing, and The Truman Show. In her free time, she enjoys exploring San Francisco. Traveling is one of her all time favorite things to do, her top 5 cities are Lille, Chicago, Le Mans, Berlin, and Brussels. The first time she traveled, she was 5 years old and her father took her to France, his previous home. When Mariam landed, she stated in shock, “I didn’t know there were this many people outside of America ''. From then on, she found a love in travel and exploring different places and cultures. She loves to search for vintage clothes, especially workwear from the 50s and 60s, Levi’s selvedge denim, and knitwear. She loves experimenting with yarn to knit and crochet. Mariam LOVES music, and is a DJ for kusf, her college radio station. She has quite an eclectic music taste, because she grew up listening to a diverse selection of music. Some of her favorite artists include Pino D’angio, Aphex Twin, Stereolab, Outkast, Pharrel Williams, Isabelle Antena, J Dilla, Madlib, Arthur Russel, Radiohead, Fela Kuti, etc… (the list goes on).
