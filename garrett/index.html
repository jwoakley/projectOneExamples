WELCOME TO MY WEBSITE


Introduction

Welcome to Garrett's website, will you will learn about his digital footprint, showcasing much of his interests and hobbies that are available online through his various accounts. Garrett has a number of accounts online that are focused on different kinds of media and art, particularly for video games, shows, and movies, along with types of journals he uses to track his activity regarding these. He also has accounts on various art websites where he has saved his favorite art across the internet for many years, showcasing his taste in characters, media, and visual art.

On this website you will find an overview of Garrett's media interests, covered thoroughly in websites like Letterboxd and Backloggd, along with examples of his thoughts and reviews of some of his favorites. You will learn of the genres and franchises that he interested in and get a good sense of what he likes and doesn't like across games, movies, books, and more, along with the frequency of his activity consuming these. On the Likes page, you will find examples of genres that interest Garrett across all media, such as psychological horror, and examples of media that fall into this category, and even examples of reviews that he has left on such media. In the Activities page you will see examples of his overall activity in terms of media logging over several years, including how much he watches or plays per month or year, and more specific logs with dates and hours.
